Introducing transpanency to the used automotive industry

Aside from  your home, the biggest investment the average person makes will be an automobile. 90 million used cars are sold every year in North America and in only 5% of those transactions are a vehicle's service maintanence records available. We want to change that. We want to inform car buyers, help vehicle owners maximize the value of their vehicle at resale time and provide automotive service industry analytics to help drive change for the better of all.
