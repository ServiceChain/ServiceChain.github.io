SERVICE CHAIN

Introducing transpanency to the used automotive industry

Aside from  your home, the biggest investment the average person makes will be an automobile. 90 million used cars are sold every year in North America and in only 5% of those transactions are a vehicle's service maintanence records available. We want to change that. We want to inform car buyers, help vehicle owners maximize the value of their vehicle at resale time and provide automotive service industry analytics to help drive change for the better of all.

Why Use Service Chain

We leverage block-chain technology to tackle the highly distributed nature of the automobile service industry. With the big players having only a combined market share of 10%, the distributed nature of block chain shines in this application. 


 Transparency into the service maintanence history of vehicles. Transacations are accurately ammended to a vehicles service history such that prospective car owners or buyers can truly have insight into the quality of care for a vehicle.

Based on the service history for a vehicle, Service chain will provide a lemon raitng for the quality of care that it recieved by comparing the manufactuers reccomended service schedule against what servicing the vehicle recieved.

How well a vehicle has been maintained has direct impact on the how long the vehicle will last. This directly translates into into the inherit value of any used vehicle at time of sale.

With Service Chain, sellers can now provide authenticated proof of service records in an easy and accessible fashion. No more collection of messy reciepts.

Car buyers now will be able to make informed decisions at time of purchase with our easy to understand lemon grading system.
